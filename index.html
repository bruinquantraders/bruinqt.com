About Us

A student-run quant trading club at UCLA. We connect Bruins with hands-on education, industry speakers, research projects, and alumni from the trading industry.

At BQT, we are focused on building a community of students passionate about quant, while pursuing professional development and building relevant projects. All students interested in math, statistics, computer science, and finance are encouraged to apply.

For industry partners and companies, feel free to reach out through team@bruinquant.com or bruinquantraders@gmail.com to connect with UCLA talent in research, trading, and engineering.
